Train Service Board

This is a display-only page. Training jobs are submitted through the API, the service executes shell scripts in queue order, and returns a train_task_id. When the job finishes, the service sends the same train_task_id back to research_mvp together with the original job information.

Queue Overview

Jobs are executed one by one in FIFO order. Under normal conditions only one job should be running, while the rest remain queued and waiting.
